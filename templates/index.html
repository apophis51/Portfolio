Flight Ticket Finder

My goal here was to make a live application, that I could practice my developer skills on. I also wanted to test the SEO viability of machine generated articles. The backend of the site is connected to the GPT3 API, and I have it set to automatically generates articles for the site with a one click button push thats programmed thats automated through python(currently 200+ generated blogs) that I fact check. The article making process consists of a scraper automated through Selenium where I get all the proper flight distances and prices.
